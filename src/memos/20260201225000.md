# Hello Moire

**What is Moire**

Moire helps you sync your Apple Notes to this website automatically.  From [here](https://docs.moire.blog) to sync Apple Notes to an online blog, save and share your thoughts.

**什么是 Moire**

Moire 帮你把 Apple Notes 自动同步到这个网站。只管写，剩下的交给它。 从[这里](https://docs.moire.blog)开始，将 Apple Notes 同步到在线 Blog，保存和分享你的思考。
